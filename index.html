About

SAFEEARTH is programmed to reward holders while increasing in both liquidity and value.


It does this by applying a

4% tax on transactions
1% goes to holders (instantly and gaslessly)
1% is auto-locked to liquidity (forever)
1% is used to buy back SAFEMARS (market buy)
1% is DONATED to charities saving Earth!


Forever Deflationary supply/burn

we will burn more than 50% of the total supply after launch and will send it to a black hole address; as this address also participates in the protocol, it accumulates more tokens, thereby effectively removing them from circulation. There is no limit to the burn, the black hole will keep growing, increasing the scarcity of SAFEEARTH.
